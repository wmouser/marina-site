Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Do you sell fishing licenses?
A; No, not in the store. You can call the Arkansas Game and Fish Commission at 501-223-6300 (or Toll Free at 800-364-4263) and order one over the phone. They will give you a confirmation number that serves as proof of your license until it arrives in the mail.
Q: Do you sell alcohol or tobacco?
A: No
Q: What do you sell?
A: We offer snacks, drinks, fishing tackle and various other things such as t-shirts, koozies and life jackets.
Q: Do you rent boats?
A: No, we no longer offer boat rentals.
Q: What are your slip rental prices?
A: Please call 479-751-2050 for pricing and availability.
