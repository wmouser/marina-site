Welcome to War Eagle Marina!

Situated near the headwaters of the White River, War Eagle Marina has been proudly serving customers for over 30 years. Family owned and operated, War Eagle Marina has all your lake needs! We offer slip rentals at unbeatable prices, gas, bait, snacks and more with the friendliest service on the lake. Open daily during the lake season.
