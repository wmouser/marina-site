Directions:

Take Highway 412 West and turn down County Road 389 towards Nob Hill. Stay left as you pass Patrick’s Hamburgers and continue heading North. There will be two more forks in the road and you will need to stay left each time. Finally, you will see a sign for the War Eagle Campgrounds. Take the left towards the campgrounds and you will soon see the gatehouse.

 

If there is someone at the gatehouse, simply tell them you are headed down to the marina. From the gatehouse, the shortest way to the marina is to head up the hill to the left past the pavilion. Continue down the other side of the hill and you will take a left at the brick outhouse building. Contiue past the gravel parking lot and you will eventually see the ramp down to the main walkway. Through the gate, head left to get to the store.

 

Hours:

During the peak season, we are open 7 days a week from 8am to 6pm.
